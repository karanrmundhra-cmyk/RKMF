Where it began · The Mundhra family

It started with our dog, Tobler.

When Tobler fell ill, we spent long nights at the vet clinic. What stayed with us was the strangers who kept coming in.

They were carrying a hurt dog or cat they’d found on the way — then learning, at the counter, that the treatment cost more than they had. These weren’t even their animals. They just couldn’t walk past one that was hurt.

“We figured the least we could do was help with the part they couldn’t.”

That is how RKM Foundation began: a family that couldn’t walk past either. Today the work is the same promise, scaled — rescue, feed, heal, shelter — and shown back to the people who make it possible.
