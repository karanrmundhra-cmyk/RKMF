## Alternate (longer — only if your tool shows a large description area and you are NOT also using the full Instructions)

RKM Foundation is an animal welfare and rescue foundation based in India. We rescue injured, sick, and abandoned animals and provide veterinary treatment, rehabilitation, sterilisation, vaccination, and adoption.

This is not a routine audit or redesign. The mission is to transform RKM Foundation into one of the most exceptional nonprofit digital experiences in the world — a website that could realistically be recognised among the best-designed nonprofit sites of 2026 for its simplicity, emotional impact, storytelling, and execution.

The site should feel simple, human, modern, premium, trustworthy, emotional, fast, and alive. A visitor should immediately grasp (1) who we are, (2) what we do, (3) why it matters, and (4) how they can help. Design philosophy is radical simplicity: every page, component, interaction, animation, and word must justify its existence; anything that adds confusion or friction is challenged, simplified, or removed. Draw on the clarity and craft of Apple, Stripe, Linear, Vercel, and Notion, combined with the emotional storytelling and trust-building of world-class nonprofits. Motion should make the site feel alive — never at the cost of performance, clarity, or accessibility.

The secondary objective is to maximise donations, recurring donations, donor trust, retention, and conversion through superior UX, transparency, storytelling, and credibility. Success is achieved when RKM Foundation is viewed as one of the most trusted, memorable, emotionally impactful, and beautifully executed animal-welfare websites in India.
