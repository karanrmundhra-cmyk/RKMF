Frame 1 — The founding truth

“Years ago, our dog Tobler fell ill, and we spent long nights beside him at a vet clinic. What we kept noticing wasn’t the other patients — it was the people at the door. Strangers, carrying a dog or a cat they’d found hurt on the road. Because they couldn’t walk past it.”
